Purple Mashed Potatoes

Home

Description

Delicious purple mashed potatoes flavored with coconut and lime.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium purple sweet potatoes, or more to taste
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Poke holes all over potatoes with a fork. Arrange in a baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until purple potatoes are easily pierced with a knife, about 45 minutes.
  4. Cut purple potatoes in half, widthwise, on a cutting board. Scoop flesh into a mixing bowl. Add coconut milk, lime juice, salt, and pepper to taste. Beat with an electric mixer until smooth.
